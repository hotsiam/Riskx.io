RISKX is a decentralised p2p risk management platform that allows users to customise smart contracts, deploy and pair them with other users of similar credit worthiness on the RISKX blockchain. Using the power of blockchain and Artificial Intelligence RISKX makes risk management simple and data driven. RISKX allows users to manually manage their portfolio, or automate management with AI and a unique bounty marketplace. The end goal is to distribute credit and risk.

RISKX works by enabling users to automate risk management for their personal portfolios or firms. Simply customise and deploy a swap contract in under 5 minutes, and mitigate an array of risk exposures that our platform detects based on user and open data. For example, you might have a 20% fixed rate on your credit card debt. Our system will pair you with the perfect counterparty that will enable you to reduce risk and increase savings.

The RIX Token is a utility token that allows users to take advantage of premium features such as Quality Spread Differentials (Rewards), Bounty programs, AI Portfolio management and platform dividends.

Contract fee (perpetual) without RIX = 0.25%. Contract fee (perpetual) with RIX = 0.05%. These fees are 100% distributed to token holders.

RISKX will be available on all major platforms, including IOS and Android.

There are no future scheduled airdrops at this stage.
